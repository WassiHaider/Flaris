› AN EXCEPTIONAL ALTERNATIVE ‹

Nowadays, time is extremely valuable. FLARIS LAR 1 goes to war with long and tiring travels and
baggage claims. It is an answer to the challenges of modern transportation. It is an alternative
to congested highways and cruise flights.

Independence

You gain a new dimension of freedom
by investing in LAR 01. Thanks to its ability
to operate on grass airstrips, Flaris allows
you to avoid large airports and get closer
to your destination point. There are over
10,000 aeroclubs and 45,000 grass airstripsn
in the world. Flaris needs just 200 m
of paved runway to takeoff and 250 m to land.
We have 124 airstrips and 62 civilian airports
in Poland, according to the Register of
Aerodromes and Airstrips (ULC) as of the 5th
of April 2019.

The Earth becomes smaller

Such flexibility is not achievable with any
other jet aircraft in the world.

Economy

LAR 01 is unparalleled in terms of purchase
and exploitation costs. Operating cost: 1,2
$/nm, or 450 $/hour

Convenience

LAR 01 meets the communication needs of
the business world. It will quickly and
comfortably get you to any destination. It is
an option for private individuals who value
their time and the comfort of travel as well.
Taking off from aeroclub airstrips allows you
to save the time spent on getting to the
airport and customs procedures. You will
also land closer to your destination.
